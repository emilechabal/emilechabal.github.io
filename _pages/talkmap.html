If you're anything like me, the pandemic will have made you nostalgic about academic and non-academic travel. In memory of better times – and in anticipation of a slightly happier future – I used some tools on this website to create a dynamic map of all the places I have given talks since 2015.

Thank you to all the lovely people, friends and colleagues, who made this travel possible!
